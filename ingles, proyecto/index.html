What is Futsal?

Futsal is a variation of football played on a smaller indoor court with five players per team. It is a fast and technical sport that requires skill, speed, and teamwork.

Basic Rules

  • 5 players per team
  • Two halves of 20 minutes
  • Smaller playing court
  • Special futsal ball
  • Unlimited substitutions
